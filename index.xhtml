I am currently a senior scientist at Rakuten Inc.. I am working mainly on market segmentation, semantic information extraction, and profiling for the finance sector. While working in the academia, my research interests were in the areas of (but not limited to) Ontology Based Data Access (OBDA) for data integration, query optimization, Knowledge Representation and Reasoning (KRR) and Natural Language Processing.
