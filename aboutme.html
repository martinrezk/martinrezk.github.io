Martin Rezk - PhD.

I was born in Cordoba, Argentina in 1982. I lived there for 18 years.... same old story, seasons passed, things happened, some reading, no religion. Life. Then I started my studies in computer science at the Facultad de Astronomia, Matematica y Fisica (Universidad Nacional de Cordoba). During that time I also started to practice a contact karate style named Kyokushin. In 2007 I got my master degree, I won a national Kyokushin Torurnament, and I got my diver license. In 2008, I started my PhD studies in Bolzano, Italy and I dived with sharks in Australia... at night. The sharks decided not to eat me. I really appreciate that. In 2009-2010 I went to Stony Brook University (USA) to work with Michael Kifer, and I also started Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. I got 3rd place in the submission only Long Island Tournament. Last but not least, I have always enjoyed taking pictures, writing, climbing, and meeting fun people. I got my PhD on April 2012, and then I came to Korea. Nowadays I continue doing the same things: working, training, and jumping from place to place, maybe to escape, just like Qin Shi Huang and his 365 rooms.
